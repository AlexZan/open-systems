Users can use their net positive/excess funds to support the cause of
their choice. From as granular as directly sponsoring a specific task in
a project, or an individual, to sponsoring a general subject.

Entities (Projects, Groups and Individuals) can all be sponsored. When
sponsoring a subject, the entities that have experience in those
subjects will earn the funds in proportion to the amount of experience
they have.

E.g. If someone is sponsoring #prostate cancer research# for 100 tokens
a month, and there is a group that has published some great research
which has 10% of all the experience in that subject, then they will earn
10 tokens for the 100 tokens monthly.
