## Key Concepts Quick Reference

### Core Principles
- **Open**: No ownership, collective benefit
- **Decentralized**: No central authority
- **Transparent**: Immutable public records (with privacy)
- **Equal Opportunity**: Power earned through contribution

### Voting & Governance
- **Experience**: Voting power earned by creating value
- **Subject-specific**: Experience in #tech ≠ power in #art
- **Consumed when voting**: Encourages meaningful participation
- **Identity-less by default**: Focus on creation, not creator
- **Fluid Representation**: Expert status can be gained/lost

### Funding Models
- **Bounties**: Open competition
- **Goals**: Milestone-based unlock
- **Projects**: Series of goals
- **Sponsorship**: Passive income from experience

### Smart Contracts
- Project, Goal, Token, Proof, Voting, Failover, GitHub Oracle contracts
- Quorum: 30% participation
- Pass: 70% approval
- Non-transferable tokens (Phase 1)
