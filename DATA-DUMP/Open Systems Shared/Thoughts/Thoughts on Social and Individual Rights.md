## Individual Rights

"The proper function of man is to live, not to exist. I shall not waste
my days in trying to prolong them. I shall use my time."

One should never have their ability to experience affected outside their
choice, except if it is breaking this right.

E.g. An individual causes trauma in another, affecting their future
ability for general experience.

## Social Rights

A global social right then should be, anyone at all times must have the
ability to change the current government. Anyone at all times must have
the ability to express any issues they have.

Thus if at any point whether a monarchy, oligarchy or any other form of
government, an individual should always have the ability to express
themselves in any way.
