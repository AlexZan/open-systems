## Contributor loop

Fund contributors choose a subject to fund, and experts (experienced
content contributors) decide which content contributors get those funds.
Funds without experts would lead to a stagnation of funds, and experts
without funds would lead to a stagnation of action.

## Experts

The world pivots with commutative experts. The sum of expert opinion
allows us to steer into a better direction. But who deems another an
expert? On 0 day there are no experts, there is no expertise. The
process of proliferating experience starts slow, but it will eventually
grow. Each time a new concept is introduced, new experts must be
developed and established to steer that concept in the best direction
for society.

## Fund Distribution

Users can choose to fund any area of interest, big or small, broad or
specific. E.g. from something broad like Cancer, to something more
specific like colon cancer, to something even broader like healthcare.

Any project that is fully open and voted in a category will gain funding
from the global funding category. E.g. A user starts a bounty to fix a
bug in the open forum. The more votes it gets for the #open tag, the
more funds it will receive as users donate to that tag or a parent tag.

### Bounty Priority

All funds will be distributed into bounties and any other remaining
funds will be distributed into royalties.

## Experience Investment and doubling experience points

The problem with this is that if someone makes an upvote with 2 votes or
more, they will already double up on half of what they consumed when
they voted, essentially giving them the entire amount of their
experience back. **Therefore voting can not be doubled, just returned.**
This way you have a net loss initially which each +2 vote made, and the
entirety of your votes must be doubled in order to recover all your
initial experience.

But if you voted very early, let\'s say 10 votes, and the votes reach
1000, you should be rewarded in some way in order to incentivise
discovering value early. Is recovering points not enough motivation to
scout? Maybe, maybe not?

Another consideration is by doubling points, the goal becomes that, to
double points, not to scout value, but in recovering, the goal is to
reward value, and with the potential for recovering experience.
