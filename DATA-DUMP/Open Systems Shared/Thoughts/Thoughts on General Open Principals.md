## Decision Tree

All decisions must be based on vote or consensus. It is suggested that
any decision based on vote (for time sensitive reasons) must eventually
be repassed through consensus (deliberation). All decisions must then
have a common structure of
problem-\>Data-\>theory-model-\>confirmation-\>proposal. Or in short
Problem-\>Data-\>Model-\>Proposal. All these points must be deliberated
before a decision is made, unless it is time sensitive.
