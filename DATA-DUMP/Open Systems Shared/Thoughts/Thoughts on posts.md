## White board concept

The ability to post content to screen real estate. This is what real
estate can govern what is most relevant, not the default time view.

Even though this is mainly client side behaviour, there are some service
behaviour and considerations.

If the white board is based off a grid system, then the service will
need to track the grid position.

For open posts grid positions need to be achieved by consensus.

Maybe the more votes a post in a whiteboard has, the more priority it
has access to, priority is governed by post size (1x1, 2x2, 4x4), and
position closer to center.

## Draft concept

If posts can be published on non block chain so that other users can
vote and comment. Draft still gives the user the option to delete it
maybe? But then the votes and comments are lost.

### Case nearly duplicate post with draft:

1.  Two posts are made and they are nearly duplicates.

2.  A user replies that the later post is a duplicate of the former.

3.  Reply is marked for attention by several users and user removes
    duplicate

#### Case Considerations

The reply would not cost anything since it is not written to the
blockchain. But the upvote on the reply would and that will not be
repaid.

## Attention concept

This will be a non blockchain action which will bring posts to
attention. This can be used in cases where a user does not have
experience to upvote, but they can bring attention to a post they think
should get higher priority to be evaluated, for example by experts, or
the original poster.

Maybe attention can be brought to specific tags. E.g. a user thinks an
#investigator is needed. Can this be exploited?

The accounts age \* total experience can be the weight of the attention
value. That way new accounts will do very little for attention spamming.

### Case Exploit:

1.  An art post is reported for #fraud.

2.  #fraud experts are selected at random and realise this post report
    has nothing to do with #fraud.

3.  The auditors don\'t pass the vote.Any auditor that might have voted
    on the non passing vote loses their experience.

##### Case Conclusion

This is exactly why reporting needs experience in the tag that is being
reported against. That way new accounts cant spam reporting. Also
exactly why bringing attention to experts who can do the reporting can
be helpful. Since bringing attention to the potential for #fraud will
not start the formal report process for fraud, but instead will notify
any auditor subscribed to #fraud to see if it needs a report. The more
attention a post gets, the higher on the attention list it is.

The attention list can be an area for expert scouts.

## Deleting a Post

Until a post gets voted it can be deleted. What will happen to replies?
Replies to a post might be valuable even if the post is deleted. In this
case. Any votes on children of the original post will stop the post from
being deleted.

Maybe even though a post is deleted, there will still be a by default
hidden record of the post and its content.

# Difference Between Post, Goal and Creation

A post contains a creation. A post can contain a creation created by the
poster, or by another individual, in violation or not.

A goal is a future creation, assessing the value of goal is assessing
the imagined value of the goals creation.

## Tags

Should users be able to post without tags? Yes, they will miss out on
the initial experience from posting with tags, but they should not be
forced to post with a tag if they do not want to. It does not remove
from anyone else\'s experience.

## Titles

Should titles be required? It would be hard to navigate a list of posts
without a title for a quick summary. Titles should be required.
