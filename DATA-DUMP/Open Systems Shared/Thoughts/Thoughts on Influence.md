## Thought: Influence Points

Influence points can be earned by any account towards a community,
whether it's a local community, venture, company, location, etc.
Influence points affect how much experience points affect a community.
They can be earned through different methods.

In a local community, influence points can be earned by checking in with
local accounts over time.

In a venture, influence points can be earned by sharing in a successful
iteration. E.g. sharing in the process of creating and selling an item.
Each iteration provides 1 point, and it is divided in proportion to the
share. So someone that has 80% share in an iteration, will earn .8 of a
point when it is completed. This might incentivise members to break down
larger iterations into smaller iterations.

## Influence and Voting

When a vote is made within a community, the vote is weighted by the
amount of influence points a user has. E.g user A has 100 influence
points, user B has 10 influence points, if they had the same amount of
experience in a vote, B would need 10 experience points to vote 10 votes
where A would only need to vote 1.
