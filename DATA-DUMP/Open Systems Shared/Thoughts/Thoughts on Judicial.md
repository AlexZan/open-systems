# Judicial

\*\* this seems to be a section out of convention, but there is no real
need for a judicial system, since it can occur organically just like
executive and legislative.

Legislative occurs when people discuss ideas pertaining to
certifications, when they vote on changes to certifications, when they
choose to use those certifications, when an institutions votes to
mandate those certifications.

Executive occurs whenever a contract requires human consensus for a time
sensitive decision, again this is not its own system or branch but
occurs organically. This is similar to judicial but the focus is on
making a decision for direction not judging guilt?

Judicial is really just a subset of executive that connects executive
with legislative, since a judicial decision becomes legislation
automatically. Maybe judicial can describe the process of auditing with
consensus of experts and jury group, as well as appeals process. In this
case its more of a Judicial Process than a system, since its a group of
systems.

I think traditionally the separation between executive, judicial and
legislative is for checks and balances to mitigate groupthink and other
biases. In the case of open systems, it occurs naturally, checks and
balances occur on the individual level, sinec anyone can participate and
everything is transparent, groupthink is naturally mitigated.

The judicial process can also be used when things go wrong with a
certificaiton/contract, when things didnt go as expected and they need
to be corrected with time sensitivity. If its not time sensitive, people
can just debate it, propose fixes, and vote in those fixes, but if its
time sensitive, we use report, audit and hot fix?

For example if someone exploits a fund, and the fund swill release in 24
hours, they are reported, audited and the funds can be returned in lass
then 24 hours, where as changing legislation (certification) alone could
never change that. Intelligent intervention is required to asses the
breaking of a certification promise, as in "i promise all evidence for a
goal is accurate and real" or something of that nature. A certification
alone can not asses that, and even if AI can, it must be the society or
community that always judges itself to maintain control over itself, and
to discover itself. A society that is managed by an AI instead can not
self actualize, some level of society must always be self actualized,
probably at the face to face level of a village or small community. Or
maybe just a community that the individual chooses to be responsible
for.

Can an individual choose to be responsible for another when the other
does not choose to let them? Yes, parent and child for example.
