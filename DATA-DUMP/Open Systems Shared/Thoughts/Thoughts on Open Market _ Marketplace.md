Example

A makerspace workshop charges 500 tokens per month per membership. To
earn a token you can buy them. Or complete tasks posted on the
marketplace. E.g. calibrate 3d printer x, must be certified in 3d
printer calibration. Other users can post jobs on the market place, such
as, I need a piece designed and made for my bike to do x and y, I am
willing to pay 1000 tokens to do so. Users can then earn their
membership by completing tasks for users.

## Should the marketplace only be in a specific token?

Maybe that should be up to the poster to set parameters of currency
type, but as well as the organization the market is part of.
