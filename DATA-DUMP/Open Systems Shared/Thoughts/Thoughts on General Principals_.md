Behaviour should be tied to an incentive. E.g. by providing users to
recoup experience when they voted early you incentivise scouts that look
for high quality content.

Besides the base multiple of double or half, numbers should always be
derived or representative. E.g. selecting 3 or more in a formula just
because it feels right, could be effective, but it will not be effective
in every case, feeling only points towards the direction of the
derivative or representative. E.g selecting 3 as a value because there
are 3 elements it is representing.
