## Voting

A user posts a definition of knitting is crafts. Another user searches
knitting to see what proposed definitions it has and sees crafts, they
agree and upvote it. A day later the vote has more than double the
downvotes and the definition proposal is accepted as a definition. If
people disagree and downvote it, as soon as the definition has less than
double the upvotes, a one day window is started for a vote to undefine
it back into a proposal. All the meanwhile a discussion can be had on
the original proposal post.
