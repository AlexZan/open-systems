# Capitalism

Dont hate on it. In its true form its very valuable to society. It is
recursive. Any wealth surplus acquired from ones earnings, should be
reinvested back into improving future earnings. The problem is when
people are irresponsible about defining their surplus. But for the most
part if a good part of that surplus is reinvested back into future
earnings, the system should function fairly well.

Allowing capital investors to earn share in a project should provide a
much greater amount of funding potential for commercial projects. A
commercial project being a project where its final product or service is
sold or traded. The individual with share will earn royalties/dividends
in all sales and/or receive initial tokens towards the product or
service.

## Example

Project with bounty begins. 20% of share could be a default value with
new projects that is reserved for capital investors. User A donates 100
dollars. They now have the full 20% potential share. So if someone was
able to deliver and complete the terms of the bounty, and the goal was
voted completed, User A would receive 20% share of dividend allocation.
The rest of the dividend shares would go to the other contributors who
also received the bounty for completing the work. User B donates 100
dollars also. Now User A and User B each have 10% share.

When the project goes live, a default value of dividend percentage could
be 5% of the purchase price or trade will go to all the dividend
shareholders.

E.g. if an open taxi service gets developed, then 5% of all fare costs
go to all of the dividend holders.

Can dividend shares be traded? Probably, they should be tokens.

## Account Sponsorship

E.g an expert can sponsor a new account who they believe has promise by
lending them experience. If the new account doubles that experience,
then it can be repaid in double or whatever agreed amount. If the new
account loses the experience, then the sponsor will not get back their
experience. The consequence of this is that a new user can be given a
headstart and contribute value to the system themselves much quicker and
if they are not able to contribute value then the sponsor is held
liable.
