This will outline a mechanism for allowing a state population to achieve
open consensus when the current government does not allow it.

A dictatorship might be in power, without the people\'s approval,
because there is no healthy mechanism to express healthy approval.

The idea then is to provide a mechanism to achieve consensus across the
entire population, that has 0 dependency to government. Therefore no
dependency to government issued id, or any other centralized
organization for that matter, since the authoritarian government is in
control of the issuing and validity of the id, and therefore could
impact the interaction with the consensus mechanism if it relied on
issued id.

The consensus mechanism must instead identify the individuals for the
sole purpose of avoiding fake users affecting the consensus. E.g. the
dictatorship creating bot accounts. Maybe there can be several consensus
mechanisms, used in different situations.

The first step is to establish a consensus mechanism, since there is not
one yet, there can be no consensus achieved on what the criteria is used
to define a member of the state. Is it that they live there? What if
they are renting? What if they know they are leaving in a few days. What
if they only arrived a few days ago? Should they have more or less
voting right than someone living there 10 years. What about those living
homeless? Maybe they were made homeless because of the dictatorship,
should they have less of a right to a vote because they do not have an
address?

The next challenges are how are the votes collected? Maybe many people
do not have access to digital devices.

The basic consensus mechanism should then have generic parameters to
begin. As long as someone is an adult

Neighbors can help neighbors. Those with devices can help register their
neighbors who do not have access to the devices. A qr code can then be
printed for the individual, which they can then scan to identify
themselves on the open network.
