A community pool of funds being grown from peers paying in from a
monthly payment (up to a limit). Once the shared limit for your vehicle
worth/liability, you no longer need to pay. The service is operated
entirely decentralized through smart contracts. If a claim is submitted
to the system, a team of auditors is selected at random amongst the
insured peers. This team must hire a 3rd party adjuster/investigator to
inspect the car and talk with the individuals involved. The auditors
must vote and agree on the cost and service of those involved, as well
as make payments and submit all financial records and receipts to the
system for all peers to review, within an established timeframe.

Auditors that agree to audit get a discount on their payments (they do
not have to pay for x months) by auditing a claim. All peers are
incentivised to find fraud by receiving a large discount to their
service, such as a 1 year discount for proving proof of fraud.

An insured found at fault in a claim will have their monthly payments
increased, or restarted. More specific rules can be defined.

In special cases such as home built, conversions, or campervans, peer
auditors would again be used to help facilitate mechanical inspections
or speciality/engineering inspections, overseeing the process. The cost
of the inspection would must be agreed upon up front and paid for by the
insured. The inspector or their establishment would be liable for any
claims made that contradicted their findings and must be insured for
liability, such as in the case of a non thorough inspection.
