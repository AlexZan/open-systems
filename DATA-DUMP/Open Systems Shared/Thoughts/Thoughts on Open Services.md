## HealthCare

### Global Health Record

A standard that is maintained through open democracy. It will create a
means for any organization or individual to gain access to a secure
record by being given an access token from the individual or an
authorized user.

### Diagnostic

An open and global service for diagnostics. Patients are able to report
symptoms and an AI or human diagnosticians can guide the patient or
authorized user through collecting the data needed to rule out causes as
a diagnosis is approached.

As the diagnostic AI studies more sample data from all patients in the
global health record, it will become more accurate and be able to notice
corroborative nuances from patient data that doctors might miss or
require a team several weeks to determine.

### Preventative

Smart devices and consumer health measuring devices are becoming
commonplace. AI can monitor health through the data collected from these
devices. Along with genetic scanning, AI can keep a close eye on any
genetic predispositions to potential health threats to mitigate or stop
them before they develop. By looking at various corroborating patterns
emerging from various devices and data sources on the patient, AI can
flag certain conditions for priority diagnosis to rule out any imminent
risks or danger.

### Emergency

Peer to Peer emergency response system. Using various devices, users can
signal that they are having an emergency. E.g. if a heart rate monitor
no longer gets a reading, it will prompt the user if they are fine, if
there is no response, the device will signal the emergency network of a
potential emergency. The system will select the best emergency
volunteers that have previously been selected to respond to emergencies
with specified emergency training, based on distance and qualifications.
An audible alarm on the device will sound to notify responders to the
area. The profile to the individual will be sent to the selected
responders with a description of what potentially occurred and a
suggested course of action. After locating the individual the responders
can carry out instructions while local emergency services are on their
way, having also been notified. In the case of specialised equipment
being needed in a timely manner, drone equipment may be dispatched.

### Holistic Care

By connecting with devices and applications which track our nutrition
and exercise, AI can also make suggestions on improving health. E.g.
When and what type of exercise to perform completely custom tailored to
the individual. What food to eat based off predicted nutritional
deficiencies, or genetic specificities.

### Community Test Centers

Eventually community funded test centers can be established in order to
measure specific data required by doctors or predictive AI. e.g. A blood
lab in a developing area can be globally funded or locally funded.

### Anonymous Data

I suggest data can be available from all global health records
anonymously, which can be a great source of training data for AI and
scientific advancement studies.

## Science

Experimental design templates can be developed and rated by the
community which can then be used to run experiments by the scientific
community. Tests can be performed and documented, reviewed, collaborated
and corroborated.

This process works perfectly with open systems as scientific members can
gain experience and thus funding in their specific fields. Tests can be
funded as bounties and studies can be funded as projects.

## Education

### Test First

I propose a test first educational system. Where a lesson is never
assumed required or helpful, but a test should accompany each lesson. If
the student passes the test, the lesson is not given, if the student
fails the test, a simpler test is given, and so on, until the student is
able to pass a test, at which point the last failed test\'s lesson is
given followed by the next failed test and so on.

For this system to function, all teaching materials must be broken down
into unit lessons that try to teach or focus on a single teaching point;
each of these unit teaching points can have multiple tests.

I propose a system for referencing unit lessons in already existing
digital educational content. Where tests can be linked to these unit
lessons through the proposed system. E.g. a video timecode that covers a
unit lesson, or a paragraph that covers another unit lesson can be
linked to several test questions.
