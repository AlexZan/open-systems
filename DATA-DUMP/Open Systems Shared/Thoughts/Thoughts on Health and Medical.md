Case developing community:

1.  Community discussion for blood analysis machine

2.  Fund created to purchase one

3.  Fund grows and able to purchase

4.  Resolution proposed with purchasing and setup details

    a.  Fund of \$10,000

    b.  Unit cost of \$8,000

    c.  Work cost of \$2,000

    d.  Must identify self and validate with 10 other users

5.  User identifies his account with picture and ID and meets 10 other
    locals that validate his account

6.  Amount transferred to user for amount on invoice from the proposal.

7.  User purchases the equipment, and brings it to medical center

8.  Proof of equipment transfer is shown with a picture and a user
    signing off on the equipment.

9.  The work cost is released

10. The medical center sets up the machine

11. A fund to created for training material and testing to authorize use
    of machine

12. The fund grows and a proposal is accepted

13. The training material and test is delivered

14. Local users begin training on using the equipment

15. The cost of different types of analysis is calculated based off the
    rates the technicians begin to offer

16. Technicians must insure themselves for the potential damage of the
    equipment in order to be allowed to use the equipment.

17. People begin to go for analysis, with one technician and one
    observer marking down a checklist from the training. Roles are
    switched every turn.
