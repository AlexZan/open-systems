Human rights certificates.

This type of certificate outlines globally defined human rights. For an
organization to maintain this certificate, it must not violate any of
the defined human rights. In the case that an organization violates
human rights, it must be made known to the population of that
organization what human right was violated, and all individuals under
that organization have the choice to either not respond about the
violations or to oppose them while maintaining anonymity. The population
that remains silent during the human rights violations will lose access
to the global community (majority of organizations) which requires the
human rights certificate. The organization will lose its human rights
certificates as well. If majority of the population within the
organization that violated the human rights opposes the human rights
violations then that should be a signal for reorganization and
retribution.

Organizations which do not have specific levels of human Rights
certification may not interact in the global community that require that
specific level of human Rights certificates e.g a nation, hence
organization that openly denies the rights of people of a different
belief or race will probably not have any human rights certification and
as a result since people in the global community should be free to
experience life equally with all others of different races and beliefs
should also be free of the interaction of any population under the
organization which supports the repressed freedoms and rights of people
of race and different belief.
