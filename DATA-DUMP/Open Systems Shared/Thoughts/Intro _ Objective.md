My original approach to explaining Open World (OW) resulted in nearly
everyone thinking it was a tech concept. Since OW **has** a tech
solution, but **is** not a tech solution, this was a roadblock in
communicating the core concept.

Like modern democracy, which uses a ballot; a paper or digital vote; the
tools that allow democracy to function, OW also uses tools, but modern
democracy is not the ballot. OW is not the discussion forum, and its
proposed tools, it is a set of ever growing social and political
principles based on an ever improving constitution.

A concept from that course really stood out. I will reiterate; That no
matter who we are as people, whether liberals, communists, facist
nazies, a warrior tribe, buddhist monks on a mountain top, or a crime
family, we have one objective in common; If we have children, no matter
what happens to us, we want the best for them. OW is about creating a
better world for our children and grandchildren.
