Accounts are a chosen identity. A user can have multiple accounts. An
account can have multiple users. Whoever the account token is shared
with has access to that account. Policy can be setup to govern an
account so that a group can vote to control an account.

E.g an account can have a policy that if more than 20 dollars of tokens
is transferred/spent a day, the next transaction will require approval
tokens from all users (the transaction can not be made without all the
users of the account forwarding their token to the user requesting the
transaction. The tokens reset after an established time and after being
used/consumed.
