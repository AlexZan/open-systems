# Social Assistance

When an individual is not able to provide for themselves, an account can
be linked to their bio identity. This will allow them to receive social
assistance under certain conditions. To make sure that the assistance is
not abused, and that there is a plan being actively followed in order
for the individual to come out of assistance. If the plan is not being
followed, and it is a matter of motivation and not abuse, a new plan
must be created in order to find motivation, or what might be affecting
motivation.
