## Forum

Allows users to post.

## Experience

Allows users to gain or lose experience from receiving up or down votes
on their forum posts.

## Reporting

Allows users to report user actions

## Auditing

Allows users who have experience in specific subjects to subscribe as
auditors to these subjects. When they are asked to audit a post or
report of that subject, they will gain experience for auditing
correctly.

## Consensus

Allows users to make decisions by acknowledging or blocking a proposal.

### Consensus Groups

Allows the formation of small temporary groups to help make time
sensitive decisions.

## Proposal

Allows users to propose executive items. If consensus approves the
proposal, the item will get added to the executive branch of the
corresponding system.
