When posting a bounty, people may vote in that they agree the bounty
will have value, but there is much less value in posting the bounty,
then actually achieving it. By posting a bounty it does not mean you
contributed the value people would vote the bounty by. The votes in this
case represent the value of completing it.

Is this the same for a goal and project? Yes since the goal and project
is owned by the poster, it is the same, the experience should go to the
owner when the goal/project is finished.
