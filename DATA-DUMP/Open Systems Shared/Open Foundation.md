Democratize everything with fluid representatives with no electoral
process. Representatives come into and out of power based on the value
they contribute to society. Power is also not binary as it is today, in
that if you are elected, you now have power, or you don't. Power is
variable, and someone can have a little of it, or a lot of it, and that
amount is always changing based on the value they contribute.

Power can be looked at as the ability to influence or affect the outcome
of an action or decision.

In order to democratize everything, everything must first be
decentralized, since by having central control to something, you allow
individuals to have control outside of the democratic process. E.g.
someone can shut down or change a service that is not in their
individual interest without voting to do so.
