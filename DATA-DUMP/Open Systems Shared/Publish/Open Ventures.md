# **Open Ventures: A Collaborative System for Income-Generating Projects**

## **Introduction**

Open Ventures is a dynamic system designed to foster collaboration on
ventures that generate income. It provides an open platform where
individuals can join and leave at any time, ensuring flexibility and
inclusivity. Compensation within Open Ventures is determined by a
collectively established concept of fairness. This document outlines the
structure, processes, and rules of Open Ventures, highlighting its
commitment to fairness and efficiency.

## **1. Membership and Workload**

### **1.1 Joining and Leaving**

Open Ventures offers individuals the freedom to join and leave the
system without restrictions, enabling flexibility and inclusivity.

### **1.2 Compensation and Workload**

Members of Open Ventures have the autonomy to choose the level of
involvement that suits them best, allowing them to undertake as much or
as little workload as desired.

### **1.3 Task Completion and Handover**

In the event that a member is unable to complete a defined task within
the specified deadline, another member can take over the task, assuming
responsibility and receiving compensation.

## **2. Task Bidding and Assignment**

### **2.1 Task Description**

For each venture within Open Ventures, project owners are required to
provide detailed task descriptions, ensuring clarity and alignment of
expectations.

### **2.2 Ongoing Bidding**

During the task bidding phase, any member can outbid a previous bid
until the bid timer expires. This allows for fair competition and
ensures the task is assigned to the most suitable member.

### **2.3 Task Assignment**

Once the bid timer expires, the task is automatically assigned to the
winning bid. At this point, no further bids can be made, ensuring a
definitive allocation of tasks.

### **2.4 Task Locking**

Upon assignment, the task is marked as \"in progress,\" indicating that
the member who won the bid is responsible for its completion. No other
member can bid on or take over the task unless the assigned member fails
to meet the deadline or abandons the task.

## **3. Payment Options**

### **3.1 Direct Payments**

Open Ventures provides members with the flexibility to choose between
direct payments and sweat equity payment options.

### **3.2 Sweat Equity Payment**

Under the sweat equity payment option, members earn compensation based
on their contributions and the venture\'s generated revenue. A profit
split determines each member\'s payout, reflecting their total earned
compensation.

### **3.3 Example Calculation**

To illustrate, let\'s consider a scenario where a member commits 100
hours to tasks with an established rate of \$80 per hour. Their total
earned compensation would amount to \$8,000. Assuming a profit share of
50% and a weekly revenue of \$1,000, the member would receive \$500 per
week until their total compensation of \$8,000 is paid, which would take
16 weeks.

## **4. Rule Sets and Certifications**

### **4.1 Open Certification Network**

Open Ventures leverages the Open Certification Network on Open Systems,
making it easy to establish rule sets for projects.

### **4.2 Template Rule Sets**

A range of suggested templates for rule sets is available, catering to
various types of open ventures. These templates offer a foundation that
can be customized to suit specific project requirements.

### **4.3 Rule Set Guide**

Open Ventures aims to develop a comprehensive Open Venture Rule Set
Guide. This guide will employ a series of questions to recommend the
most appropriate rule set based on a venture\'s unique characteristics.

### **4.4 Fairness and Human Rights**

To ensure fairness, open ventures can subscribe to certifications that
emphasize human rights and fairness. In the event of a reported
certification breach, the community can impose sanctions until the issue
is resolved. This mechanism promotes self-governance and upholds
fairness within the Open Ventures ecosystem.

### **4.5 Sanctions and Certifications**

Open Ventures recognizes the importance of maintaining fairness and
upholding human rights within its community. To ensure accountability,
open ventures have the option to subscribe to certifications that
prioritize these values. In the event of a reported certification
breach, a thorough verification process is conducted.

If a certification breach is confirmed through the verification and
ruling by the community, the implicated open venture must address the
issue promptly. Failure to do so may result in sanctions being imposed.
These sanctions extend not only to the open venture itself but also to
community members who engage in business with the non-compliant venture.

Community members who also subscribe to certifications promoting human
rights and fairness are required to sanction their business against the
non-compliant open venture until the issue is properly addressed. This
self-governing mechanism ensures that open ventures remain accountable
to the principles they subscribe to and promotes a fair and ethical
environment within the community.

By adhering to certifications and upholding fairness, open ventures
establish a trusted ecosystem where members and customers can
confidently engage in collaborations, knowing that their rights and
values are respected and protected.

## **5. Task Review, Disputes, and Consensus**

### **5.1 Task Completion Review**

Project owners review submitted work to assess its alignment with the
task definition and established rule sets. If the work meets the
required standards, the task is marked as completed, and the associated
payment becomes due.

### **5.2 Task Rejection and Fixes**

In instances where the submitted work does not meet the established
standards, the member responsible must address the identified issues and
make the necessary fixes, adhering to the task details and rule sets.

### **5.3 Dispute Resolution**

Should disputes arise between members and owners concerning task details
or rule sets, a formal debate process is initiated to address the
matter. The objective is to reach a consensus through community
involvement and deliberation.

### **5.4 Consensus Building**

Active participation from the community is crucial in the debate process
to foster consensus. The community collectively weighs different
perspectives, striving to find mutually agreeable resolutions.

### **5.5 Core Rule Set**

In the event that a consensus cannot be achieved, the core rule set of
Open Ventures serves as the governing framework. This rule set may
include provisions to automatically resolve disputes, such as randomly
favoring either the member or the owner involved. This approach promotes
fairness and ensures that all parties maintain an equitable stance
throughout the debate and consensus resolution process.

Open Ventures empowers individuals to collaborate effectively, pursue
income-generating projects, and uphold principles of fairness. By
leveraging its flexible framework, members can contribute their
expertise, create value, and participate in a self-governed ecosystem
driven by shared goals and objectives.
