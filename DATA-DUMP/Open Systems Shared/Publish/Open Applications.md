# Application & System Proposals

Once there is a proven core ecosystem of applications, further derived
systems and applications can be created. I will list out the systems
which I have created specification documents for and which I think will
provide great social value.

While some of these applications and systems might seem like things in
the distant future or radically advanced, they are things that will be
possible today with the use of open systems. Many of these systems are
already being worked on, and through open projects they are able to be
developed quickly and concurrently.

## Healthcare

Since projects can now be developed and maintained through open
processes, this opens the door to systems that can handle very sensitive
individual data such as health records, with no risk of those records
being used to control the individual by a centralized organization that
might have access to that private individual data.

### Health Record

All data is transparent, but anonymous, so the data can be used in
health studies, but it can not be used to do anything on an individual
basis. In order for an individual to provide their private data to a
doctor or hospital, it must be an explicit action through an access
token that expires.

This as usual is all governed through certification, and users can
access whichever health service they are comfortable with based on their
certifications.

Through the use of open systems, we can achieve a universal health
record which will open the door to overall improved well being.

### Diagnostic

Since the health record is open but anonymous, AI can openly train on
this data and achieve a high level of diagnostic accuracy that would be
available to all universally at extremely low cost or free. Areas of the
world that had no access to modern diagnostic health care now can.

### Clinic Example

A village in a developing part of the world that has access to the open
platform can express that they have an issue with diseases. Users can
set up a goal to fund the development of a diagnostic clinic in the
area, which will plug into the diagnostic systems, using the advanced AI
systems to diagnose through the clinics rudimentary tests which are
enough to diagnose the most prevalent diseases in the area.

### Preventative

Smart devices and consumer health measuring devices are becoming
commonplace in the developed world. AI can monitor health through the
data collected from these devices anonymously. Along with genetic
scanning, AI can keep a close eye on any genetic predispositions to
potential health threats to mitigate or stop them before they develop.
By looking at various corroborating patterns emerging from various
devices and data sources on the patient, AI can flag certain conditions
for priority diagnosis to rule out any imminent risks or danger. The
Diagnostic AI services can even request further tests to rule out and
pinpoint diseases potential.

### Emergency

I propose a peer to peer emergency response system. Using various
devices, users can signal that they are having an emergency. E.g. if a
heart rate monitor no longer gets a reading, it will prompt the user if
they are fine, if there is no response, the device will signal the
emergency network of a potential emergency. The system will select the
best emergency volunteers that have previously been selected to respond
to emergencies with specified emergency training, based on distance and
qualifications. An audible alarm on the device will sound to notify
responders to the area. The profile to the individual will be sent to
the selected responders with a description of what potentially occurred
and a suggested course of action. After locating the individual the
responders can carry out instructions while local emergency services are
on their way, having also been notified. In the case of specialized
equipment being needed in a timely manner, drone equipment may be
dispatched.

## Education

Educational content creators will be able to earn passive income from
successful lesson or course creation through the standard sponsorship
and value economy. The more value the educational content creates, the
more experience and passive income the content creator earns. If the
content is used in a commercial work, the set revenue share % can also
be observed as with most other creations.

### Test First

I propose a lesson should never be assumed to be required, but a test
should accompany each lesson and If the student passes the test, the
lesson is not given, if the student fails the test, a simpler test is
given, and so on, until the student is able to pass a test, at which
point the last failed test\'s lesson is given followed by the next
failed test and so on.

For this system to function, all teaching materials must be broken down
into unit lessons that try to teach or focus on a single teaching point;
each of these unit teaching points can have multiple tests.

I propose a system for referencing unit lessons in already existing
digital educational content. Where tests can be linked to these unit
lessons through the proposed system. E.g. a video timecode that covers a
unit lesson, or a paragraph that covers another unit lesson can be
linked to several test questions.

#### Testing

Results from tests can be shared on an account in order to display a
user\'s knowledge level in various subjects. This is different from
experience which is creating value with knowledge/skill. This might be
useful when being considered for a job or a project.

#### Retention

Doing a subject refresher sometime after the initial study can easily be
done through test first, quickly zeroing in unit lessons forgotten,
while reinforcing the passing tests.
