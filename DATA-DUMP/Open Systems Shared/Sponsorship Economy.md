1.  User X has a passion for space exploration, so they donate 50 dollar
    a month to #space exploration.

2.  On the other side of the planet, user Y comes up with a concept for
    an engine design that could help space exploration, they post this
    to the forums.

3.  Users begin to upvote the concept, another user proves that
    mathematically it could work with a reply.

4.  Users create an open project to create a prototype

5.  Users start to upvote it with many tags, including #space
    exploration

6.  Around the time X's monthly donation is made, The open project has
    1000 points in #space exploration, which is roughly 0.04% of the
    total share of #space exploration.

7.  As a result, 0.04% of X's monthly donation goes to the fund of the
    open project

User X could have searched on his own for the project and manually
funded it, but with potentially thousands of projects, this could be
very time consuming. By donating to a tag instead of a project users can
have their donations made automatically in proportion to the highest
ranked projects and funds.
