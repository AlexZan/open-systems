## Voting with the nets funds

People are essentially voting with other peoples funds. This essentially
disconnects monetary wealth from power in this regard. Though users can
always withdraw their contributions, there might be some holding period
to make this less exploitative. If a vote goes a different way then
expected by that of the funder, they can not immediately withdraw to
reduce the capability of the votes action. Although this generally will
not be the case as either an action is voted as to be supported or not.
A vote is generally not between two actions.

## Capital

If capital must be acquired, no one or no service will have access to
that capital. The only way for capital to be withdrawn is through a
community voted smart contract which will get used by the service and
for the purpose it was intended for prior to gathering the capital.

Ideally the only time to gather capital is if it offers an advantage to
a utility that wouldn\'t be available without that capital. E.g. as in
an transitory insurance company that takes monthly payments and saves
them in a holding, incase of a payout. Transitory is explicitly because
even the function of insurance can be replaced by a non capital method.

Therefore

Capital can only be held when it offers a clear advantage to a service
that could not be achieved in any other way, other than through holding
capital.

## Early Investors

#### Bids for Manufacturing and Delivery

At this point a Beta Bounty in each region can be started. This bounty
is a bit special in that, you are not donating, rather reserving a beta
unit with the set amount u contribute to the bounty. E.g. \$300 beta
cost.

When the beta bounty in a region reaches a certain point, manufactures
or brokers will start bidding for the work. The community will vote on a
manufacturer.

Since this is a large project, a manufacturer will be expected to
provide milestones that the bounty will break down into with quality
assurance every step of the way from presenting their progress to the
community.

Another option is a token sale, with a limited number of tokens. The
problem with a token sale is that it involves capital. There will be a
pool of funds either in the manufactures hands, an intermediary, or
hypothetically OW's. Since the idea is the avoid capital in all cases
where there is no disadvantage to doing so with another solution, this
might go against that principal.

Both reserving a beta phone and buying a token rewards early investors
and incentives early investment, though with a reservation you get the
phone soon as it is manufactured, where as with a token, you get it only
when u redeem the token, meaning the phones value must be held from the
time of token purchase. With a token you can exchange it anytime for a
phone, but problems arise. What if the manufacturer no longer wants to
make the phone and the capital for the tokens is held by them. Second
what if a new phone is made, next years model maybe, should the original
token holder have access to that new phone? What if an investor
purchases 100 tokens so that they can sell them for double the value,
does this behaviour contribute to the net?

# Commercial Ventures

Causes will typically be non-profit, but they might also result in a for
profit cause, which we can call a venture. A venture is controlled by
the owner. An owner may or may not choose to create a coin for their
venture. The coin value will increase or decrease with the value of the
venture, and any coin holders, as well as the owner can also make a
profit. Users can also hold on to their coins and use them towards the
product or service once the venture goes live, since every venture coin
must have a utility towards the venture product or service, such as; the
product is sold for venture coins, or a service can be paid for with
venture coins.

For example:

A user has a great idea for a product, and they require funding to
produce it. There are many options to make this work:

1.  The user can make the product open source, with a license that
    limits anyone else from selling it or manufacturing it for sale.
    Although users can manufacture the product for personal use. This
    way users can assist in contributing to the growth or development of
    the product as they wish. The product owner can manually award coins
    if they like, or automatically by creating sub causes in the venture
    with a bounty they set using the venture coins.

2.  The user can seek funding for a product idea, or prototype they have
    by creating an Initial Coin Offering and marketing the idea or
    prototype. Users can then buy the venture coins directly with other
    coins such as bitcoins, hoping that they will see a return as the
    venture develops and increases in value.

3.  The user can create a milestone for venture funding. They can
    request funding upfront before each milestone, or as a reward for
    completing each milestone. Requesting funding as rewards for
    milestone will likely be better funding since the owner acknowledges
    they will complete each milestone with their own funding.

4.  There are also other ways to fund a venture that the full white
    paper will cover.

# More Specifics

## Vote Actions

Besides upvoting and downvoting a post there are several actions you can
perform. Some are mutually exclusive while some are not:

1.  UpReward/DownReward - used when you think a post should earn a
    bigger or smaller portion of the bounty.

2.  Emoji - this is used to indicate you find a post funny, sad,
    upsetting, etc. They have no effect on upvoting,downvoting, or
    bounty. They are simply used to express an emotion. These are used
    more for posts that do not end up becoming funded causes, which Open
    World also has support for.

## Vote Tokens

When a cause is successful, by contributing to it, you also get voting
tokens specific to the tags of the cause's topic, proportional to the
amount of votes your contribution reply achieved.

For example. If there is a cause that has the biology tag, and you made
a popular contribution to cause. When the cause is successful, not only
will you get a good part of the bounty, but you will also get biology
tokens. Let's say you get 20 biology tokens, now if you see a cause that
you are passionate about, instead of just up voting once, you can upvote
it 21 times instantly. Since you are deemed more experienced in biology,
you have more voting power in the subject.

These tokens are also listed on your profile. If you choose to make it
public, users can view what you have experience in by looking at the
different type of tokens you have, and the total amount of tokens you
have in each subject.
