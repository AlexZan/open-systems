## Free Speech

1.  No policy against any speech

    a.  Hate speech will be community regulated

2.  Unless;

    a.  Explicitly expresses intent in illegal activity

    b.  E.g lets murder John Doe, Let's rob John doe

## No ownership

1.  Any service within the system can not have ownership. There can be
    founders of the service and/or concept for due credit, but no one
    will have any special or extra privilege, control, rights or
    entitlement to the service.

2.  A cause or concept that gets funded or support through OW **can**
    have ownership, but if that concept becomes a service and integrates
    with OW, it must not have ownership.

## Decentralized

1.  OW is not a system, but a doctrine; a set of principle based
    policies.

2.  OW is not a legal entity

3.  OW has no central operation, central location or central authority

4.  OW has no authority, no ownership

5.  OW has decentralized systems that operate on OW principles and
    policies

## Equality

1.  No individual is to have any type power, privilege or authority over
    any other individual

    a.  No administrators or moderators

## Capital

1.  Capital can only be held when it offers a clear advantage to a
    service that could not be achieved in any other way, other than
    through holding capital.

2.  If capital must be acquired, no one or no service will have access
    to that capital. The only way for capital to be withdrawn is through
    a community voted smart contract which will get used by the service
    and for the purpose it was intended for prior to gathering the
    capital.

## Non Profit

1.  No individual may profit off OW unless it is through a cause.

    a.  An individual may earn income from donations made to a cause
        they contributed to or donations made directly to them.

    b.  An individual may earn income from an achieved bounty in a cause
        they contributed to.

2.  A cause can support a for profit business or objective

    a.  A cause can support anything imaginable, and it is up to the
        community to allow it or moderate it

## Transparent

1.  Any service within the system must have all records accessible for
    the public.

    a.  Anonymous accounts will maintain anonymity even if referenced in
        a services public record.

    b.  Anonymous posts referenced in the service will not be linked to
        any account

2.  Any service within the system must have all code and digital assets
    open source and accessible to all

## Secure Identity

1.  One account per person, fake or multiple accounts violations will
    have consequences

2.  Accounts cannot be created without an invitation.

3.  The user inviting the invitee is responsible for their behaviour

    a.  If a user invites a fake account, that will be addressed through
        policy

    b.  If a user has a pattern of inviting problematic invitees then
        that must be addressed through policy
