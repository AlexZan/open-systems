## Objective

This document is meant to help guide society in creating a decentralized
democracy platform which I hope will eventually lead to a decentralized
global nationless state.

Where nations define cultural differences, but not government,
militaries and economies. Where nations can help people experience
different cultures and people, but never repress them or restrict
anyones experience.

This document is meant to compliment the decentralized platform now
released @ link. The app has the basic functionality needed for
decentralized democracy through an open forum.

## Open Forum

This is where users go to discuss any subject, problems, ideas. This is
the heartbeat of Open Democracy. The quality of deliberation is
proportional to the quality of consensus. Consensus should always be the
favored, and majority vote saved for time sensitive decisions.

## Sponsorship Economy

You choose what to put your excess funds into. By sponsoring a subject,
project, group, individual.

## Governance Protocol

A global governance protocol created and maintained through open
democracy.

### Rule of Multiple Identities

Anything that a user may do by creating a second account should be
allowed with a single account. Multiple accounts should never allow a
user to exploit the system, or provide an advantage over a single
account.

### Development

The development of open systems, open source designs

## Industry

Creating products and services in the physical realm, through the demand
of open source designs.

Each industry will have its own profit split protocols, and users can
take part in whichever they think is fair to them.

E.g a user comes up with an updated profit split contract for the music
industry. As artists become more familiar with the new contract, they
begin to use it and find it is more fair. The users of the contract then
upvote the contract, which provides the experience to its creator.

## Overall

Value creation will be the incentive, not the accumulation of capital.
