Open source project for a device that is similar to a table, but with a
matrix of soft point rods. Maybe the table is human sized, and the rods
will be about 1/4 inch in thickness, and as many as needed to fill the
table.

An actuator for each pin can allow it to travel up its entire length or
a set length at controllable speeds and power.

The table will allow for a matrix scan of the body, by actuating the
rods up until there is contact with the body and it will measure the
resistance for the remainder of travel up to a preset final resistance.
It can perform this with preset routines such as a wave pattern which
can detect any knots in the body. It can then target the knots through
more programmed routines. AI can be used to find patterns in statistical
success both qualitatively and quantitatively as well as develop new
routines based on its findings.

More advanced units can include a blanket of rods that is flexible to
contour the body, this way a 3d matrix scan of the body can be achieved.
A compression mechanism can provide resistance to contour the blanket,
and then the process can begin.

The rods can be conductive to provide TENS functionality as well.

The device can feature disinfection features in between uses such as UV
light and steam clean.
