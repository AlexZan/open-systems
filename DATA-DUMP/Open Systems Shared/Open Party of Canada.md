# Canadian Specific Proposals

### Spending & Budget

All spending decisions will require an open debate. All local spending
(city level) will require local voting to pass from registered voters.
The entire process must be transparent. Budget must be transparent. If
there is left over budget, it must correlate to a reduction in local
taxes for the next fiscal. This requires a mechanism for reduction in
local taxes, not provincial taxes. Maybe a heatmap of spending per
capita.

Case:

Fix Hurontario Road potholes

1.  Proposal for a specified length

2.  Cost analysis and contract bid breakdown

3.  Vote from all locals to proceed or not

New Park

1.  Proposal for a park from a designer, with cost analysis and contract
    bid breakdown

2.  Vote from all locals to proceed or not

## HealthCare

### Deductible cost

All healthcare services will have an initial deductible cost which
increases once a threshold is passed (set by the yearly average). This
is to reduce the system cost of light frequent visits.

### OHIP Payment Reform

Set pay for ohip clinics that is only reduced by passing a threshold of
reports and ticket backlogs. The clinic is not compensated for each
visit. This way doctors can now take their time and attempt to resolve
as many issues in one visit as they can to reduce the current overhead
of multiple visits.

### Comprehensive Care

Ohip should cover every healthcare related service, including mental
health care.

### Preventative Care

Personalized plans for disease prevention. Higher upfront cost for a
later reduction in emergency care.

## Police

### Tiered Services

There will no longer be a general police force, but a multi tier police
force.

### News

It will not be permitted for any government fund, or funds of any
government affiliates to be given to any news agencies. Government
should have no involvement in news.

In order for news to be deemed open, it must have completely open books,
be non profit.
