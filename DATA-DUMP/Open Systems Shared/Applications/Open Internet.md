An internet service system using open systems principals. The system
will comprise an application to handle supply and demand of internet
service.

Users in areas that do not have adequate internet options can submit a
demand ticket. Areas that have high demand tickets are likely to attract
internet suppliers. Demand tickets can include a deposit and a monthly
subscription fee they are ready and willing to pay for the service. This
information will provide suppliers with all the information they need to
determine their profits.
