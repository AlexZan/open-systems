## Urban Transforming Vehicle

A vehicle with a small physical signature that will turn into an upright
riding platform (think segway) when riding indoors or in high populated
areas, at slow speeds, that transforms into a 3 wheel rider with
comfortable seat for longer distance high speed travel. Can be parked
vertically to save space. E.g. fit 5 vehicles into one car parking spot
with the ability to summon vehicle independently. Autonomous features
such as summoning, collision avoision, driving when in 3 wheel position.

# Domestic

### Veggie Barter

A system which allows growers of veggies to exchange for other veggies.
E.g. one banana for 2 tomatoes. A free market where the exchange value
using "veggie coin" is established organically through past sales and
open orders. The veggie coin is non transferable by default as to avoid
the commercialization of veggie growing, to avoid the prioritization of
profit over health.

E.g. a hydroponics user that grows basil in his condo, has an extra 50
grams they would like to trade for 100 vegcoin. They list it on the
market. Someone willing to come pick it up i.e. a neighbour will accept
the trade. They will agree to a time, and the neighbour will pickup the
basil. When the veggie is marked as picked up, the veggie coin is
transfered.

#### Auditors

Users with experience in the field can be voted as auditors after they
apply. They will receive vegcoin per audit, and a bonus if they can
provide evidence of rule breaking

#### Rules

The community will set and manage the rules of vegibarter. The rules
will ensure healthy and safe produce as well as trades.

#### Comercial

A user can make a commercial venture on veggiebarter, but they must do
so through a commercial account, there by making the vegicoin
transferable.
