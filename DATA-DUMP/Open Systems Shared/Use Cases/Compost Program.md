1.  Identify Compostable rules

2.  Food scraps and other organic waste can be exchanged for organic
    tokens

3.  Compost is drained and weighed, then an equivalent amount of organic
    tokens are transferred by scanning the user app with service app.

4.  Organic tokens can then be redeemed for fertilized compost, and
    vegetables grown by anyone who accepts organic tokens.

5.  Tokens can not be transferred except for exchanges through the app.
    This lets local communities know that what they provide as compost
    will be used to grow the food they will eat next season, and so they
    are incentivized this way to ensure quality and safety of compost.

# Stages

1.  Compost to tokens and Tokens to fertilized compost

2.  Veggie barter through tokens
