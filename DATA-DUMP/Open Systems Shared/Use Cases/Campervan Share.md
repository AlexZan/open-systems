A user is working on a campervan. They want to fund it with a token
sale. Each token can be worth 1 day of vehicle rental. Therefore the
investors will only be local within a few hours drive. The user also
offers to pay tokens if he receives help in building it.

#### How many tokens to issue?

If there are 10 tokens issued, then someone could get all 10, and hang
on to them and no one else will be able to rent it, unless they buy the
tokens from the one person.

If 1 million tokens are issued, will it take the value down? In this
case no, since the value is tied to the one day rental value.

Therefore an unlimited supply of tokens can be issued?

#### Tokens for work

Users can help build the campervan for tokens. A list of tasks can be
bid on by the workers. The owner selects the worker based on their
experience/skill and bid amount. E.g to paint the vehicle I bid 50
tokens.

Maybe to spread the word, just for showing up you get a token.

#### Early Discounts

The first 200 tokens can be sold for \$50 each, then the next 500 can be
\$75, and the remainder can be \$100 per token until its completed.
Tokens will then be issued at the going market rate for renting a
campervan which is about \$150-200 per day.
