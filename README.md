## Core Principles

**Open** – No one owns the system. Value creators own their creations, but the system belongs to all participants collectively.

**Decentralized** – No dependency on central servers or authorities. Decisions and data are distributed through blockchain-based ledgers.

**Transparent** – All actions, votes, and transactions are public and traceable. Personal privacy is preserved through cryptographic privacy.

**Equal Opportunity to Power** – Voting power (called "experience") is earned only through creating value for others. It cannot be purchased or inherited.

**Value = Voting Power** – Contribution determines governance influence.

**Truth = Transparency** – Immutable records prevent revisionism.

**Progress = Collective Creation** – No single authority decides outcomes.
